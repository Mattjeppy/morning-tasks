# DevOps and The Cloud
​
Do some research to answer the following questions in your own words as best as you can.
​
1. What is DevOps?
​
a set of practices, principles, and cultural philosophies that aim to streamline and integrate the processes of software development and IT operations. 
​
2. What are the 3 ways of DevOps and what do they mean in your own words?
​
Practices of flow - focused on the flow of working through the system, from initial concept to deployment(left to right)

Practices of feedback - Feedback from operations through to development and back again in an almost infinite loop of application development.

Practices of continual learning and experimentation - Capture the value of the first and second way of creating a culture of continual learning and experimentation.
​
3. What do you think a DevOps Engineer does as part of a team?
​
The goal of DevOps is to improve the efficiency, collaboration, and quality of software development and delivery. Effectively bridge the gap between software development and IT operations. Streamlining the software development lifecycle and improve overall efficiency of development.
​
4. What do you think the most important qualities of a DevOps or Cloud engineer are?
​
-Understanding of programming languages and concepts from front-end to back-end.
-Security awareness
-Working as part of a team 
-Problem solving skills
-Technical proficiency with platforms and integration.
